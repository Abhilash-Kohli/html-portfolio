Best Movies

Top korean crime/mystery thrillers

Memories of murder

"Memories of Murder," directed by Bong Joon-ho, is a gripping blend of crime and dark comedy, unraveling a real-life unsolved murder case in 1980s South Korea. Stellar performances and masterful direction create a captivating exploration of justice and obsession, making it a must-watch cinematic gem.

I saw the devil

"I Saw the Devil," directed by Kim Jee-woon, is a relentless Korean thriller that explores vengeance through the intense cat-and-mouse game between a secret agent and a sadistic serial killer. With outstanding performances and expert direction, the film is a chilling and impactful exploration of justice and morality.

Oldboy

"Oldboy," directed by Park Chan-wook, is a gripping Korean thriller that follows Oh Dae-su's quest for revenge and redemption after 15 years of inexplicable imprisonment. With a dark and unpredictable storyline, outstanding performances, and masterful direction, the film seamlessly combines intense action with psychological depth, creating a thought-provoking and unforgettable cinematic experience.
